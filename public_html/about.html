About Us

Life is too short to debate where to eat.

Probably Confucius

We believe that while online food services like Yelp and Grubhub have made the process easy..they haven't made it easy enough. We have been provided with far too many options, dooming the average American to analysis paralysis. But fear not. We're here. We're here to tell you where to eat (especially when you want tacos...because who doesn't want tacos?).

We hope you enjoy using our service, and if you have any thoughts or suggestions please don't be a stranger. Stay hungry, friends.
